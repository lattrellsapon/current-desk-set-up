Although I am a software developer graduate, I never really had a desk set up while studing at university. Hence why the machine that is powering this set up is a laptop. During my time at university, I did all of my work on my laptop only. I just found it convenient as I could just take it anywhere I wanted to go.

The initial reason why I wanted to have own desk set up was so I could be productive and I could practice the skills I learnt at uni. My Macbook Air is also just 13 inch in size, so I wanted to have another monitor to look at to increase productivity. Lastly, I also got hooked into watching set up videos on YouTube.

2013 Macbook Air

The machine that is powering this set up is a Macbook Air that came out in 2013. I love this laptop as it lightweight and the MAC OS is so much easier than Windows. I find that I am more productive with a MAC OS.

24 Inch Samsung Monitor

Since I am programmer, an external monitor is a must. It helps so much with productivity as I get to have multiple screens to look at while working.

E-Blue Mechanical Keyboard

I love this keyboard as it has RGB lights. I do not game as of right now, but I just like the way they look.

Cooler Master Mouse

This mouse also has RGB lighting which matches my RGB keyboard.

As you can tell by now, I am going for a minimalistic set up. Therefore, cable management had to be on point.
